**expr-lang/expr v1.17.8**. Date: 2026-06-09.

## Verdict

**Maximum Parity substantially met** at the level realistically attainable in
TypeScript: every upstream file is mapped, every upstream testcase is
classified, every divergence is documented and traced to a forced JS/runtime
cause. All 9 parity reports are present and consistent.

"Full Parity Achieved" is **NOT** claimed — 3 NOT_IMPLEMENTED_YET items remain
(see FINAL_ACCEPTANCE.md #10–12). These are maintenance-convenience gaps, not
functional or behavioral gaps.

It is **not byte-for-byte bytecode parity** — the collapsed arithmetic helpers
and the dropped typed-func dispatch are documented DESIGN_DECISIONs (zero
behavioral effect), and reflection-dependent strict checking is a
FORCED_DIVERGENCE.
